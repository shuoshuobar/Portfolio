Telling stories with data

Yushuo is currently a graduate student in Data Journalism at Columbia Journalism School. Born in Beijing, China, she started as a student journalist since high school and minored in Journalism and Statistics while serving as a staff writer for her undergraduate newspaper. Beyond traditional journalism writing, she is a big fan of magazine or feature writing. You would be amazed at her enthusiasm for bitter matcha ice cream and raw, sour fruits. In her spare time, you can find her playing badminton, watching live band shows and scribbling notes on her phone for her public, Substack-like WeChat account.
